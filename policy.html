Artist Verification Policy

1. Verification Requirements

To receive an official verification badge, you must:

  • Have a public GitHub profile
  • Provide your GitHub username during signup or profile update
  • Have a minimum of 1,000 followers or 100,000 total plays
  • Maintain authentic and active profile

2. Verification Process

Our system will automatically verify eligible artists by checking their GitHub profile. Once verified, a badge will appear on your profile and tracks.

3. Verification Removal

We may remove verification if you violate our policies or if your profile becomes inactive for 6 months.
